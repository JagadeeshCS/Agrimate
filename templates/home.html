AI Assisted App for farmers on Agri Practices & Suggestive Crops

CROP RECOMMENDATION

The crop recommendation system leverages machine learning algorithms to offer personalized crop suitability recommendations. It considers diverse environmental factors such as soil type, climate, precipitation, temperature, humidity, and pH levels. The system analyzes historical agricultural data and utilizes predictive models to provide tailored recommendations specific to individual farms or agricultural regions.

WEATHER FORECASTING

The weather forecasting feature provides accurate predictions using advanced meteorological models and algorithms. It forecasts various weather parameters such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation. Users can access real-time weather updates and forecasts for their location. The system offers interactive visualizations and alerts to help users plan outdoor activities and make informed decisions based on weather conditions.

AGRIMATE-CHATBOT

The system employs AI-driven chat-bot technology to provide interactive guidance and assistance to farmers. It offers personalized advice on crop selection, pest management, irrigation scheduling, and other agricultural practices. It assists farmers in accessing agricultural knowledge and expertise, making it easier to address farming challenges and optimize productivity. Additionally, the Agribot feature enhances accessibility by providing support via various communication channels such as text messaging, web chat, and mobile applications.

SCHEMES

The "Schemes for Farmers" feature provides comprehensive information about government-sponsored agricultural schemes and programs aimed at supporting farmers. It offers details on subsidies, grants, loans, and incentives available for various agricultural activities such as crop cultivation, livestock rearing, and farm infrastructure development. Users can access eligibility criteria, application procedures, and benefits offered by different schemes. The feature helps farmers stay informed about the latest agricultural policies and initiatives, empowering them to avail themselves of financial assistance and resources for improving their farming practices and livelihoods.
